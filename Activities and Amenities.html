Activites and Amenities

Somes Sound View Campground offers our guests several activities and amenities to make your camping experience comfortable and convenient.

Kayak Rentals to our guests Paddle Boat free for use in the Quarry Boat Mooring rentals
Picnic tables and fire pits at each site
Clean restrooms with hot showers and flush toilets ($1 for 5 minutes of hot water) - tokens purchased at office Water and electricity hook-ups.
Cabins Wooded, Water view & waterfront sites
RV sites, Platforms, Boat dock, boat launching facilities Salt water fishing Rocky shore ocean swimming Heated swimming pool free to our guests
Playground Nearby Hiking, Biking, Lake swimming,
