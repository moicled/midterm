SSVC Rules & Regulations

Management reserves the right to ask campers who are not abiding by our rules and regulations to vacate their site with no refund. Please note that it is up to you to read and be aware of all of our rules and policies.

Cancellation Policy:

Because of our small and seasonal business, cancellations affect us significantly. Therefore we adhere to a very strict cancellation policy. Cancellations made more than 14 days prior to arrival will result in the refund of one half of your payment. Cancellations made within 14 days of your confirmed arrival date will result in forfeiture of the entire payment regardless of the reason.

Reservations are recommended for July and August, or for a favorite site. Please notify campground office if you are going to be arriving later than 8pm at 207-244-3890. Must be 18 years of age to rent a campsite. Check in time is after 1:00pm. Check out time is by 11:00am sharp! We reserve the right to remove your property from the campsite if you have not vacated by 11:00am on agreed departure date. *Quiet time starts from 10pm to 7am and is enforced. Please respect other campers and keep your noise level to a minimum, otherwise the police may be called and you will be asked to leave.
No loud parties will be tolerated at any time. No firearms allowed in the campground. This includes BB guns etc. Radios and televisions are to be kept at a reasonable level. Heaters and Air conditioners may be used but we ask that you only use them when necessary. Turn them off whenever possible – Please THINK GREEN!
There is absolutely NO SWIMMING in the Quarry, this is for your own safety. The Quarry is very deep with many sharp and dangerous rocks. Please observe the 5 mph speed limit on camp roads. Do not walk through other campsites. Respect other campers.
Dispose of all trash in dumpsters provided for your convenience. Please recycle bottles in containers provided (no trash in these containers, please). Leave your site as you found it … CLEAN. Your card will be charged a $25 fee if you move/tamper with fire pits or trash the campsite in any way.
Limited supplies are available at the campground office/store. Management is not responsible for any lost or stolen items. There is a lost and found in the office. Local wildlife is very friendly. Securely store food and anything else a large raccoon could carry off!
Picnic tables, a water faucet, and fire pits are provided for each site. Please leave something on your site so we know it is occupied and not for rent.
Please report any problems to the office or call 911. Please note that local law enforcement patrol the grounds regularly. Please abide by all Maine State laws.
