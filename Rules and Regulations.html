SSVC Rules & Regulations

Management reserves the right to ask campers who are not abiding by our rules and regulations to vacate their site with no refund. Please note that it is up to you to read and be aware of all of our rules and policies.

Cancellation Policy:

Because of our small and seasonal business, cancellations affect us significantly. Therefore we adhere to a very strict cancellation policy. Cancellations made more than 14 days prior to arrival will result in the refund of one half of your payment. Cancellations made within 14 days of your confirmed arrival date will result in forfeiture of the entire payment regardless of the reason.

Payment Requirements: We require full payment prior to your arrival, also see the cancellation policy. A $10 fee will be charged due to insufficient funds on all returned checks. In the event of a returned check final payment, plus fees, will be due immediately.Reservations are recommended for July and August, or for a favorite site. Please notify campground office if you are going to be arriving later than 8pm at 207-244-3890. Must be 18 years of age to rent a campsite. Check in time is after 1:00pm. Check out time is by 11:00am sharp! We reserve the right to remove your property from the campsite if you have not vacated by 11:00am on agreed departure date. *Quiet time starts from 10pm to 7am and is enforced. Please respect other campers and keep your noise level to a minimum, otherwise the police may be called and you will be asked to leave.
Visitors Must check into the office, park within the host campsite and leave before quiet hours,10:00pm. No loud parties will be tolerated at any time. No firearms allowed in the campground. This includes BB guns etc. Radios and televisions are to be kept at a reasonable level. Heaters and Air conditioners may be used but we ask that you only use them when necessary. Turn them off whenever possible – Please THINK GREEN!
Pool Rules: Must rinse off before entering pool. Do not touch fittings. Children under 16 years of age need to be with an adult. No running, diving, smoking, dogs, food or drink in pool area. We keep the pool open in nice weather from 8am to 8pm unless otherwise posted. We reserve the right to close the pool for poor weather or for maintenance.There is absolutely NO SWIMMING in the Quarry, this is for your own safety. The Quarry is very deep with many sharp and dangerous rocks. Please observe the 5 mph speed limit on camp roads. Do not walk through other campsites. Respect other campers.
Dispose of all trash in dumpsters provided for your convenience. Please recycle bottles in containers provided (no trash in these containers, please). Leave your site as you found it … CLEAN. Your card will be charged a $25 fee if you move/tamper with fire pits or trash the campsite in any way.
Limited supplies are available at the campground office/store. Management is not responsible for any lost or stolen items. There is a lost and found in the office. Local wildlife is very friendly. Securely store food and anything else a large raccoon could carry off!
Picnic tables, a water faucet, and fire pits are provided for each site. Please leave something on your site so we know it is occupied and not for rent.
Please report any problems to the office or call 911. Please note that local law enforcement patrol the grounds regularly. Please abide by all Maine State laws.
Dogs: We do allow friendly dogs. Please leave your dog at home if he/she is not good on a leash, around children or barks incessantly. We do not allow dogs in any of our cabins or in the pool area. Please be responsible with their waste and properly pick up after your pets. Dogs must not be left unattended and must be on a leash at all times. Please be aware that if your dog(s) are acting aggressive, excessively barking or making other campers uncomfortable you will be asked to leave the campground with no refund.
Children Parents are responsible for the behavior and safety of their children in the water, boat ramp, dock, pool and in the camping area. Children riding bikes need to wear helmets!
Moorings Available for rent at $30 per night. Contracts must be signed before use in the office.
Fires/Wood: Do not bring any wood from out of the area into the campground. Don’t give bugs a free ride! We have firewood available to buy at the camp store. Cutting trees or breaking branches off trees is prohibited. Campfires must be extinguished prior to being left unattended.
Site Occupancy Limitations: Each site is designed for 1 tent/RV/camper or van, 1 vehicle, and 4 people. An additional tent, vehicle or adult may be added at a cost of $5 per night if space allows. Consult SSVC staff.
Cars All cars in the campground must display a valid car tag. Vehicles that do not have one are subject to towing at the owners expense. Cars are not to be left on the shoreline unless you have a waterfront site, they’ll need to be parked in the designated spot on your campsite. We do not have a parking lot, only a few spaces for guests checking in at the office. Extra cars will be charged $5 per night.
Bath House Washing dishes, clothes, dogs or port-a-toilets is not permitted in the restrooms or showers. Showers run on tokens which can be purchased at the office; $1 for 5 minutes of hot water. You may also get a refund for unused tokens at the office. Please turn showers off when you are done and note there is a delay for the water to stop after you have turned to the off position. Please clean up after yourself in the bathhouse and report any issues to the office.
Kayak Rentals Kayaks are available for rent in the office to use in Somes Sound. Paddle boats are free for use in the Quarry, remember children must wear life jackets. Contracts must be signed before using and you do so at your own risk. Life jackets are required and provided for your safety. No use during bad weather or after dark.
